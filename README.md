## 📋 Module Summaries

### Task 1 – Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic *(Module 1 | 20 marks)*
Created a one-page infographic using **Canva** covering: what digital literacy means, safe internet practices, professional online presence, and useful digital tools for students. The visual was designed for a student audience and exported as a PNG.

### Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio *(Module 2 | 20 marks)*
Set up professional profiles on **GitHub**, **LinkedIn**, and **Kaggle**. Created a GitHub Profile README with name, branch, year, and learning goals. LinkedIn Education section was filled with current degree details. Screenshots of all three profiles are included.

### Task 3 – Coding & Collaboration Platforms *(Module 3 | 20 marks)*
Completed a beginner challenge on **HackerRank** (Solve Me First). Built a **Google Form** with 5 questions as a Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz for batchmates, including multiple choice and short answer questions.
🔗 [Digital Literacy Quiz – Google Form](#) *(link to be added)*

### Task 4 – Professional Email & Etiquette Guide *(Module 4 | 20 marks)*
Drafted two professional emails: (1) requesting a deadline extension from a professor, and (2) expressing interest in a summer internship to a coordinator. Also created a Social Media Do's and Don'ts checklist with 10 points for responsible online behaviour.

### Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness Case Study *(Module 5 | 20 marks)*
Researched **Phishing** as the chosen cybercrime type. Wrote a 200–250 word case study covering how phishing works, who it targets, and its consequences. Created a "Stay Safe Online" checklist with 10 actionable tips, including UPI/financial safety and reporting via **cybercrime.gov.in** and helpline **1930**.
